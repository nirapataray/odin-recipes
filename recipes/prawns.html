Prawns

Home

Ingredients

  • 500g large prawns (peeled and deveined)
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic (finely minced)
  • 1/2 lemon (juiced)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley (chopped)
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional for heat)
  • Salt and freshly cracked black pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Peel and devein the prawns, leaving the tails on for better presentation.
  2. Pat the prawns completely dry with a paper towel so they sear properly in the pan.
  3. Melt two tablespoons of butter with a splash of olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Add minced garlic and optional red pepper flakes, cooking for 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. Add the prawns in a single layer and cook for 2 minutes without moving them.
  6. Flip the prawns and cook for another 1 to 2 minutes until they turn pink and opaque.
  7. Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the top and toss with finely chopped fresh parsley.
  8. Remove from heat immediately to prevent the prawns from becoming tough and rubbery.
