Lasagna

Home

Ingredients

  • 500g lean ground beef
  • 250g sweet Italian sausage
  • 1 large yellow onion (finely chopped)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 800g crushed tomatoes (canned)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 12 lasagna noodles (oven-ready or boiled)
  • 450g ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 400g mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • 50g Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley (chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef and Italian sausage in a large pot, then drain the grease.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, garlic, and herbs, then simmer the sauce.
  3. Mix ricotta cheese with one egg, fresh parsley, and a pinch of salt in a bowl.
  4. Boil the lasagna noodles until al dente, then drain and rinse with cold water.
  5. Layer meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella in a baking dish.
  6. Repeat the layers until the dish is full, ending with a thick layer of mozzarella on top.
  7. Cover with foil and bake at 190°C (375°F) for 25 minutes, then uncover and bake for 25 more.
  8. Let the lasagna rest for 15 minutes before slicing so the layers hold together.
