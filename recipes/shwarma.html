Shwarma

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken thighs (boneless and skinless)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt and black pepper (to taste)
  • Pita bread, garlic sauce, and pickles (for serving)

Steps

  1. Marinate the meat in garlic, yogurt, lemon, and spices for at least 3 hours.
  2. Roast or pan-fry the meat over high heat until the edges turn golden and crispy.
  3. Shred or thinly slice the cooked meat into bite-sized pieces.
  4. Warm up your pita bread or flatbread on a hot skillet.
  5. Spread garlic sauce or tahini generously across the middle of the bread.
  6. Add the sliced meat, pickles, onions, and fresh tomatoes.
  7. Roll the flatbread tightly and wrap the bottom in foil to hold it together.
